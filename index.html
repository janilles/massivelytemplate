It's a simple idea

We take your kindly donated instrument – be it old or new, proudly used or painfully neglected – and put it into the hands of musicians and songwriters whose passion and potential are limited only by the fact that they don’t have good instruments of their own.

Donate an instrument

When you donate an instrument, you’re not “just” giving us an instrument. The ripple of your donation will reach a lot more people than you may think!
