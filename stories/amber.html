An acoustic bass
for Amber

Here’s a short story about how magic happens.
And how YOU can be a part of it.

Take a few people with a bit of time and passion — in this case, five — one long bus journey and an unused bass guitar. Here’s how it starts:

Step 1. Someone reaches out. In this case, Sue, the very proud mom of a very talented young musician, Amber. Amber needs a bass guitar. Sue drops us a line.

Step 2. Someone begins the search. In this case, Jan. He finds Marella, who’s selling her old and no-longer-used bass guitar on Gumtree. Would she consider a deep discount for Amber?

“Sure, sounds good!”

Marella comes up with a price that could easily turn people with a few quid to spare into happy mystery donors. And it does.

Step 3. Someone makes the instrument playable and enjoyable. In this case, Jan again, who repairs the guitar.

Step 4. Someone takes the guitar on its five-hour journey to Amber’s doorstep. (Thank you, Steve!)

Step 5. Someone gets happy. In this case, Amber! Happy Amber = more music in the word.

Which someone are YOU?
