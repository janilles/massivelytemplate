Donate an instrument

The Shed is the perfect new home for your unused or outgrown musical instrument. We’ll give it a once over or fully restore it to its former glory – whichever is needed – and then put it in the hands of a musician who’ll get its unique voice back out into the world.

When you donate an instrument, you’re not “just” giving us an instrument. The ripple of your donation will reach a lot more people than you may think!
